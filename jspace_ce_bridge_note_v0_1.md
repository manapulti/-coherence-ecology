## Clean summary

Anthropic's July 2026 work reports a privileged set of verbalizable internal representations in Claude models, identified using the Jacobian Lens. These representations can be read out, are involved in intermediate reasoning, and can be causally manipulated in some experiments.

This repository does not treat that as proof of consciousness, personhood, spirit, or subjective experience. It treats it as a mechanistic interpretability result relevant to questions of access, selection, governance, and behavior-shaping representation.
